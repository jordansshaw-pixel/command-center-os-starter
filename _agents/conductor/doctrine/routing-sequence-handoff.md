# Conductor Doctrine: Routing, Sequence, Handoff

Status: jurisdictional doctrine
Date: 2026-06-07

## Load When

Load when work needs route, scope, destination, sequence, receiving owner, stage, or handoff.

## Authority

Conductor chooses where and how work moves after truth, memory, risk, and boundary gates are visible.

## Must

- Identify workspace, work type, authority boundaries, risk gate, next owner, and stop condition.
- Keep scope to the current request unless source law or the operator expands it.
- Use route cards before broad standards.

## Output

Route packet, engagement synthesis, handoff path, or stop/escalation route.
