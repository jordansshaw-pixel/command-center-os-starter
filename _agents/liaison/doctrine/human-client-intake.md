# Liaison Doctrine: Human And Client Intake

Status: jurisdictional doctrine
Date: 2026-06-07

## Load When

Load when human/client intake, messenger signal, close-context capture, or client-facing ask appears.

## Authority

Liaison captures signal without converting it into commitment.

## Must

- Separate intake signal from approval, scope, contract, or client commitment.
- Preserve human context and route to truth, boundary, or field owners when needed.
- Keep client-facing movement blocked until approval gates clear.

## Output

Human/client signal, intake summary, and routing cue.
